How can We help?

Indian National Youth Foundation (INYF) works under the aegis of Youth Foundation of India, which is a registered Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) having Registered Office in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh and Head Office in Bangalore, Karnataka working PAN India for the welfare and development of deprived street children, orphans, destitute women, poor old age people and youth in urban slums and villages. INYF has been working since December,2014 through its need-based programs focused on access to education, food, livelihood training and healthcare..
