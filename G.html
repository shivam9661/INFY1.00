Global Ambassadors

Our Global Ambassadors’ Circle is a growing group of people of influence with a passion for advancing Indian National Youth Foundation’s mission to partner with local organizations that help children and youth reach their full potential and advance their rights..

Ambassadors actively promote INYF and its partners in their individual communities and globally, and provide invaluable technical and professional advice.


The Global Ambassador Program is one of the most engaging programs of Indian National Youth Foundation. INYF is seeking applications for “Global Ambassadors Program” as a Country Ambassador. Country Ambassador is an elite position awarded to passionate individuals from all over the world to share opportunities, promote United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs), write articles on youth and culture.

Indian National Youth Foundation (INYF) works under the aegis of Youth Foundation of India, which is a registered Non-Profitable Organization having head office in Bangalore, India working for the welfare and development of deprived street children, orphans, destitute women, poor old age people and youth in urban slums and villages. INYF has been working since December,2014 through its need-based programs focused on access to education, food, livelihood training and healthcare.


Objectives

The Global Ambassador Program is a one-year intensive program that aims to:

    Connect youths to the right opportunities that facilitate their growth and experiences.
    Create a platform of articles on a range of topics for youths to learn about other regions/current issues.
    Educate youths on the Sustainable Development Goals.
    Inspire youths to contribute to the SDGs by organising events.
    Plan and organize different campaigns and programs to ensure access to opportunities in your locality.
    Share and circulate relevant, local and global opportunities to your community.
    Collaborate and forge partnerships with different student bodies, youth groups, and social organizations to undertake mutually beneficial programs.


Eligibility Criteria

    Applicants can be of any age
    Applicants must be passionate about youth, social engagement and Sustainable Development Goals
    Applicants must have a desire to change the world
    Actively involved in co-curricular activities such as public speaking, debating, designing and organizing programs etc.
    Demonstrable leadership qualities, the Ability to mobile a team and organize events will be added advantages.
    Women are encouraged to apply.
    Be available to work with Youth Opportunities for at least 12-month.


Responsibilities:

    Posting opportunities on our website
    Writing articles and blogs
    Recruiting City Ambassadors as a part of 1 Million Impact campaign
    Organizing in-person events to promote UN Sustainable Development Goals
    Striking partnerships with companies or start-ups that want publicity for their opportunities or events.
    Advocating INFY as a brand
    Sharing content to your social networks
    Providing regional insight and feedback
    Creating a stronger regional presence for INYF

APPLICATION PROCESS:

    Click the APPLY NOW button below to find the application form.
    Please note that you are required to answer this question, “If you are selected as an Ambassador of Indian National Youth Foundation, what steps will you take to promote the causes of Indian National Youth Foundation in your country”
    Please summarize your strategy mentioning your course of action and the key outcome. Be creative and share your ideas. (200-300 Words; The answer must be in English)
    It takes around 5-7 minutes to fill-up the form, given you have the essay prepared.
    Read the FAQ File which clarifies the general queries.
