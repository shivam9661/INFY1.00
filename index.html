Indian National Youth Foundation

Working as one for the good for many

Indian National Youth Foundation (INYF) works under the aegis of Youth Foundation of India, which is a registered Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) having Registered Office in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh and Head Office in Bangalore, Karnataka working PAN India for the welfare and development of deprived street children, orphans, destitute women, poor old age people and youth in urban slums and villages. INYF has been working since December,2014 through its need-based programs focused on access to education, food, livelihood training and healthcare.

We encourage you to read about our programs and support us by volunteering and donating. Popular causes are INYF’ Cloth Bank, INYF’ Food Bank, Destitute Elders People Midday Meal Program, Distribution of Educational Materials to Poor Children and Sponsor a Child Program.

Please read about projects and donate generously for a cause close to your heart. You can donate online through secure payment gateway with your credit/debit card or net banking account or through Paytm, Paypal, Phonepe and UPI.
